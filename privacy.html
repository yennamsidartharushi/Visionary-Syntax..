Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Welcome to Visionary Syntax. Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Visionary Syntax, we may collect basic information to help our website run smoothly:

  • Voluntary Information: Information you provide directly, such as your name or email address when you contact us through our Support Portal.
  • Automated Information: Basic, non-personal data collected automatically by our servers, such as your browser type, device type, IP address, and the pages you view while on our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

3. Understanding Cookies

Visionary Syntax uses "cookies"—small text files stored on your device—to remember your preferences (like your choice of Dark or Light mode) and to understand how you interact with our website. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

We use third-party services to analyze traffic and display advertisements.

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website.
  • Opting Out: You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

5. Data Protection

We take the security of your data seriously. We use standard industry practices to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of your personal information.
